What TimeCell covers

Framework Part 1–2
Temperature Gauge

MVRV + RHODL composite. 0–100 scale. Zones: extreme fear to extreme greed. Context for every entry decision.

Framework Part 3
Ruin Test

BTC −80% AND other assets −40% simultaneously. One scenario. Pass or fail. No ambiguity.

Framework Part 4
Conviction Ladder

Six rungs from Observer (0%) to Single-Asset Core (50%+). Gates and requirements for each level. Know exactly where you stand.

Framework Part 5
Position Sizing

Six-step process grounded in geometric mean CAGR. Hedged vs unhedged returns across cycle lengths. Break-even hedge analysis.

Framework Part 6
Selling Rules & De-Risk Triggers

Pre-written triggers for when to take profits. Removes emotion from exit decisions. Defined in advance, not in the moment.

Framework Part 7
DCA Calculator

Dollar-cost averaging across temperature zones. Buy more in fear, less in greed. Systematic, not emotional.

Framework Part 8
Sleep Test

Scenario: BTC drops 80% tomorrow. Can you stay the course? The only metric that predicts whether you'll actually hold.

Framework Part 9
Downside Insurance

Hedge budget, payoff math, break-even cycle length. How much insurance is worth buying — and when it starts costing more than it protects.

AI Layer
Action Plan

AI-generated summary of your current position and recommended next steps. Grounded in all framework components. Personalized to your numbers.
