Framework Depth

Temperature Gauge

MVRV + RHODL composite. 0–100 scale. Zones from extreme fear to extreme greed. Context for every entry decision.

Crash Survival

BTC −80% AND other assets −40% simultaneously. Runway in months across four drawdown scenarios. Pass or fail.

Conviction Ladder

Six rungs from Observer (0%) to Single-Asset Core (50%+). Gates and requirements for each level. Know exactly where you stand.

Position Sizing

Grounded in geometric mean CAGR. Hedged vs unhedged returns across cycle lengths. Break-even hedge analysis.

Selling Rules

Pre-written triggers for when to take profits. Removes emotion from exit decisions. Defined in advance, not in the moment.

DCA

Dollar-cost averaging across temperature zones. Buy more in fear, less in greed. Systematic, not emotional.

Sleep Test

BTC drops 80% tomorrow. Can you stay the course? The only metric that predicts whether you'll actually hold.

Downside Insurance

Hedge budget, payoff math, break-even cycle length. How much insurance is worth buying — and when it costs more than it protects.

Action Plan

AI-generated summary of your current position and recommended next steps. Grounded in all framework components. Personalized to your numbers.
