# Moonshots
layer: pack

## Goal
Small bets, huge upside. Asymmetric opportunities where the downside is capped but the upside is 10-100x.

## Goal Type
range

## Target
- min: 0% of portfolio
- max: 5% of portfolio
- unit: percent
- source: pack
- reasoning: "Never more than 5%. Each individual position should be sized so losing 100% of it doesn't matter."

## Eligible Assets
- Altcoins with strong thesis
- Early-stage crypto projects
- Angel investments / VC funds
- Pre-seed/seed equity
- Options (long calls, defined risk)

## NOT Eligible
- Anything requiring > 1% of portfolio per position
- Leveraged positions
- Illiquid assets with lockup > 2 years

## Key Metrics
- total_moonshot_allocation vs 5% cap
- individual position sizes
- win/loss ratio (tracked over time)

## Special Rules
- Individual position max: 1% of total portfolio
- Write-off assumption: invest only what you can lose entirely
- No averaging down — if thesis breaks, exit

## Provenance
source: pack
reasoning: "Moonshots are the fun money. Asymmetric bets that might 100x but probably go to zero. Size accordingly."
