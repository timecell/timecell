# Growth Engine
layer: pack

## Goal
Compounds quietly. Diversified growth assets that complement the core BTC position.

## Goal Type
range

## Target
- min: 5% of portfolio
- max: 20% of portfolio
- unit: percent
- source: pack
- reasoning: "Some diversification beyond BTC for traditional growth. Not the main bet, but a steady compounder."

## Eligible Assets
- Index funds (S&P 500, total market)
- ETFs (broad market, sector)
- Individual stocks (blue chip)
- Bitcoin mining stocks
- Yield-bearing stablecoins (with guardrails)

## NOT Eligible
- Speculative altcoins (those go in Moonshots)
- Illiquid investments
- Leveraged products

## Key Metrics
- allocation_drift (target vs actual)
- performance vs benchmark

## Provenance
source: pack
reasoning: "Even Bitcoin maximalists benefit from some non-BTC growth exposure. This bucket catches traditional compounding."
