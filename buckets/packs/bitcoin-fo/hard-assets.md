# Hard Assets
layer: pack

## Goal
Tangible, inflation-proof. Real assets that hold value across cycles and provide optionality.

## Goal Type
range

## Target
- min: 0% of portfolio
- max: 20% of portfolio
- unit: percent
- source: pack
- reasoning: "Physical assets diversify beyond financial markets. Real estate, gold, and collectibles have different risk profiles than crypto/equities."

## Eligible Assets
- Real estate (residential, commercial)
- Gold (physical or allocated)
- Silver
- Art and collectibles
- Agricultural land

## NOT Eligible
- REITs (those go in Growth Engine — they're financial instruments)
- Commodity futures (leveraged)
- Cryptocurrency (digital, not tangible)

## Key Metrics
- total_hard_asset_allocation
- individual property valuations
- rental yield (for income-producing properties)
- maintenance costs

## Special Rules
- Illiquidity is a feature — these are long-term holds
- Valuations updated annually (not daily)
- Mortgage leverage is acceptable (unlike BTC leverage) but tracked

## Provenance
source: pack
reasoning: "Hard assets are the 'sleep well' portfolio. They don't 10x but they don't go to zero either."
